Primary School Monitoring Timetable

For Academy Committee Link Member

This timetable provides a structured approach for Academy Committee Link Members to monitor key areas in a UK primary school, ensuring statutory requirements are met and pupil needs are prioritized. The schedule is organized by term and includes ongoing responsibilities.

Autumn Term

Key Activities
  • Meet with school leaders to review the School Improvement Plan (SIP) and set monitoring priorities.
  • Review safeguarding, health & safety, and SEND policies for statutory compliance.
  • Attend induction/training for new governors or link members.
  • Conduct a site walk-through to identify any immediate risks or needs.
  • Review pupil premium and catch-up funding plans.
  • Attend the first Academy Committee meeting of the year.

Spring Term

Key Activities
  • Meet with subject leaders to review curriculum delivery and pupil progress.
  • Monitor the impact of interventions (e.g., phonics, reading, maths support).
  • Review attendance and behaviour data, focusing on vulnerable groups.
  • Check progress on actions from previous term's monitoring.
  • Attend Academy Committee meeting to discuss findings and next steps.

Summer Term

Key Activities
  • Review end-of-year pupil outcomes and progress data.
  • Meet with SENCO to evaluate SEND provision and impact.
  • Check health & safety and safeguarding arrangements for summer activities and trips.
  • Attend final Academy Committee meeting to review the year and plan for the next academic cycle.

Key Features

  • Statutory Compliance: Ensures all legal and regulatory requirements are met throughout the year.
  • Pupil Focus: Monitoring activities are designed to prioritize pupil safety, wellbeing, and progress.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Regular reviews and meetings maintain high standards and accountability.
  • Reporting: Structured reporting to the Academy Committee for transparency and improvement.
